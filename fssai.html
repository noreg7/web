The Chemistry of Adulteration

From synthetic paneer to pesticide-laden spices, industrial chemicals are infiltrating domestic supply chains.

Synthetic Paneer Synthesis

Step 1

Urea, Detergent, and Palm Oil are emulsified with water to mimic milk's white liquid appearance.

Step 2

Sulfuric or Hydrochloric Acid is added to rapidly precipitate solids from the synthetic milk analogue.

Step 3

The mixture is pressed into sheets and packaged in unhygienic, clandestine units for local caterers.

The Ethylene Oxide (EtO) Crisis

International recalls (Hong Kong, Singapore) of MDH and Everest spices triggered a 40% export risk. EtO is a Group 1 carcinogen used as a sterilization gas.

Regulatory Clash:

In April 2024, FSSAI raised the default MRL for unspecified pesticides tenfold (0.01 to 0.1 mg/kg), drawing backlash from health advocates.
